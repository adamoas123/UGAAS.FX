1. STRATEGY

(Qorshe ama strategy shameystiran oo shaqeysa lana tijabiyey)

Strategy waa nidhaam ama qorshe ladego muddo dheer ama muddo kooban si loo hormariyo ama guul looga gaaro. Waxaa jira nidhaam loo maro waxwalbo larabo in guul laga gaaro ama lahormariyo, hadey noqoto ganacsi, hormar shaqsiyadeed, siyaasad, dhisin wadan, hogamin, gaaris dhaqaale maliyadeed, kobcin dhaqaale, kuwasi waa tusaaloyin cad oo markana ubahan strategy. Hadaba suuqan forex, waa suuqa ogu weyn aduunka dhanka sarifka malinlaha ah. Hadaba si lamid ah waa inan heysana nidham aan marka ogu guleysan karno waana strategy. Hadaba maxaa meshan noga cad, iney waajib tahay inan heysano qorshe marka aan ku hool gali karno, si aan lacag ogu sameyno suuqa.

2. TRADING PLAN AMA ROUTINE

(Qorshe ama jadwal shaqo)

Trading plan waa qorsheynta malintada ama sida aad uwajihi laheyd suuqa, sida taxliinta guud malinta, qorida lacagaha aad rabto manta inaad fursada karadiso, ilaalinta shuruucdada strategy, indho kuheynta fursadaha waqtiga ay diyaar noqonayan, gabagabeynta malinta sidey aheyd inta adan seexan ka hor. Routine waa jadwal kudegsan aad suuqa soo gasho waxeyba kuxirantahy nooca adiga tahay. Ani ahaan waxaan ahay Day trader, waxaana badna trade gareeyaa New York session laga bilaabo 4:00pm East Time ilaa 9:00pm. Inta badan waxa intaa noo diidey ama naga hortagan ayna dadka badankii u arkan iney caadi tahay qofka malinkii uu rabo inuu trade gareeyo ama uu iska maqnan karo, ama ha trade gareeyo ikn uusan suuqa diirada saareynin kudhaxwada holihiisa kale noolol maalmedka ahaa, sida dawasho social media, lashakeysi asxaab, hurda uu arko inuu seexdo caadi ay latahay, caweys, ama signals raadis uu umaleyo inuu sida lacag kusameyn karo. Intaa qodob aan sheegey waa kuwa dhalinta ay kudhibanyihin una diidey iney ka guuleystan suuqa.

Faa'iidooyinka inaad Trading Plan La Hato:

  • Inaad noqoto trader fiican shuruudisa ilaaliya lacag sameynta waa natiijo.
  • Dhisinta iyo xakameynta fikirka maskaxda kudhasha xiliyada khasaraha iyo faa'iidada.
  • Inaad sameysato xili suuqa u gooni ah waxey ka cawineysaa inaad isku aragto qof horey usocod ah.

3. RISK MANAGEMENT

(Xakameynta Khasaaraha)

Ganacsi walbo wuxuu leeyahay qasaaro iyo faa'iido intaba. Trading kana waa ganacsiga ogu weyn aduunka dhanka sarifka aan aniga kashaqeysano sarifka lacagaha qalaad (forex). Malinkii keli ah waxa suuqa lakala sarifaa lacag kudhawad $7.5 trillion USD ah, taasi oo kutuseysa awooda ganacsi aad rabto inaa barato waxaana ogu horeeya ganacsatada waweyn sida bankiyada caalamka. Su'aasha waxey tahay ma umaleysa meel si fudud lacag looga sameeyo ama fikir shaqsi ani iyo adi ah iney si fudud ogu guuleysan karan? Jawaabta waa haa ama maya heerka aqoneed adiga aad leedahay. Si ay jawaabteena haa ay unoqoto hadaba waa in aan barana xakameynta qasaraha, madama suuqa ay nagula jiraan BANKS iyo dadyow kale oo naga awood badan suuqana ukaxeyn karan dhankey raban u go'aamin karan kor ama hoos. Hadaba waxaan kusinayaa Table aad raaci doonto aan aniga xita si malinla ah uisticmaalo ina fududeyey inaa suuqa faa'iido kala imado.

4. JOOGTEYN, SAMIR IYO ADAAB

(Consistency, Patience, Discipline)

Bani aadam ahaan waxan u abuuranahay degdegsimo nafeed.
